Biography

I'm a third year Ph.D. student at the University of Arizona. My advisor is Dr. Carlos Scheidegger. I also worked with Dr. Richard T. Snodgrass. My current research interests are big data visualization, exploratory data analysis, machine learning and HCI. I also have research experience in computer vision and machine learning during my master's program.

I received my M.S. in Computer Science from Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in Beijing, China and my B.E. in Software Engineering from Northeast Normal University in Changchun, China.
